Mailing Lists

Mailing lists for padb discussion and development are available for public use, if you are an existing user or are considering using padb for the first time I would advise you to join.
  • padb-devel (archives) for discussion of ongoing development of padb, feature requests and technical discussion
  • padb-users (archives)for discussion on the use of padb and general questions.
